WELCOME TO ASC！

The Applied Security & Cryptography (ASC) Research Lab, directed by Prof. Zhe Liu, focus on network security, data security, system security and cryptography. The lab seeks to develop theories, techniques, and systems to enable a more secure and efficient information systems, that are more robust, accountable, and privacy preserve. Our current research focuses on six areas: (1) Crypto Engineering, (2) Cryptography algorithms, (3) Software and System Security, (4) AI Security, (5) IoT Security, and (6) Blockchain security and Privacy.
