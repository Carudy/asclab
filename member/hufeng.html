About:

Feng Hu is currently a postdoctoral fellow at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. From March to September 2018, he went to the University of Houston to visit and study. Currently engaged in research work on drone colonies, spectrum sharing, and privacy protection. He published 3 SCI journal papers as the first author, 3 CCF recommended conference papers, undertook and participated in 5 projects above provincial and ministerial level, and applied for 3 invention patents.

Research Interests:

  • Drone Colonies
  • Spectrum Sharing
  • Privacy Protection
