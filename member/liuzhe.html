About:

Zhe Liu is a professor and doctoral supervisor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. He has published more than 100 papers (more than 40 top-level papers) in journals and conferences in this field, of which the most important conference in the field of cryptographic engineering is on the international CHES Published 4 academic papers (in the past 20 years, Chinese scientific research institutions as the first unit published a total of 10 papers on CHES). With his scientific research achievements in the field of cryptographic algorithms and Internet of Things security, since returning to China in 2018, he has been selected into various national and provincial and ministerial talent programs 13 times and received peer-reviewed academic awards, including thousands of national young people, China Association for Science and Technology Young Talents Support Project, Jiangsu Computer Society Youth Science and Technology Award, and Alibaba Badamo Academy Green Orange Award (2019 Jiangsu Province and Ministry of Industry and Information Technology colleges and universities are the only selected, a total of 10 people nationwide), etc. He currently serves as an editorial board member of seven authoritative computer security academic journals such as IEEE Internet of Things Journal and Journal of Cryptographic Engineering, and has served more than 100 times as a program committee member of international conferences on computer security and cryptography conferences, including INSCRYPT 2019 and CryptoIC 2019 Chairman of the program committee of the meeting.
