About:

Ge Chunpeng's main research directions are cryptography, information security, and cloud data security. He hosted and participated in the National Natural Science Foundation of China, hosted the National Postdoctoral Foundation, participated in the National Natural Science Foundation of Australia, and the Natural Science Foundation of Jiangsu Province. A total of 31 SCI papers were published, of which 12 were published as CCF Class A or B by the first author or corresponding author. The paper googled him more than 420 times, and the h-index was 11. He won the 13th International Conference on Network and System Security (5th International Symposium on Security and Privacy in Social Networks and Big Data) Best Paper Award. In addition, he served as a program committee member of ACISP 2020, IEEE GlobeCome 2020, DSC 2019, NSS 2019, ICC 2020, Inscrypt 2019, CryptoIC 2019 etc.
